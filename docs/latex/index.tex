 Welcome to GIDE, the Guilds Integrated Development Environment, brought to you by The Nameless Guild!

\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md1}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Overview}{Overview}}\label{index_autotoc_md1}
GIDE is an ambitious project at its early stages, aiming to provide a robust and feature-\/rich Integrated Development Environment tailored for the needs of Guilds. Developed using C++ and Qt, GIDE is designed to be a powerful tool that facilitates seamless collaboration, code organization, and efficient development within the Guild ecosystem.\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md2}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Key Features}{Key Features}}\label{index_autotoc_md2}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Fast and Lightweight\+:}} Its aims to be fast and lightweight, as it uses C++ and Qt.
\item {\bfseries{Easy Setup\+:}} Will setup environments with a click of a button, and will have many templates to built projects from.
\item {\bfseries{Early Stages\+:}} GIDE is in its early stages of development, and we invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Your feedback and contributions are invaluable in shaping the future of GIDE.
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md3}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Getting Started}{Getting Started}}\label{index_autotoc_md3}
